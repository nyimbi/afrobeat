  but is constrained by slow production timelines and budget limits. AI
  can automate and accelerate the process.

  languages, including Spanish, Mandarin, and French, expanding the
  audience.

- **Global streaming demand for African stories** is at an all-time
  high, with Netflix, Amazon Prime, and others actively investing in
  African content.

  year** could flood the market at a fraction of the current cost,
  achieving mass profitability.

#### **Projected Profit Model:**

- Producing **100 AI-generated Nollywood films** at \$500K each = \$50M
  cost.

  revenue**.

- **Projected profit = \$150M.**
