Features

Everything a
creator needs.

Gbẹdu isn't a toy generator. It's a full production environment for African music.

🎙️

Real singing, not speech

ACE-Step 1.5 produces fully sung vocals with correct rhythm, melody, and emotion — not robotic text-to-speech. The vocals breathe, syncopate, and harmonise like a human singer.

🌍

15+ African language lyrics

Generate lyrics in Yoruba, Nigerian Pidgin, Igbo, Hausa, Twi, Swahili, Lingala, Wolof, Amharic, Zulu, Shona, Darija Arabic, and more — with authentic idiom and cultural references.

🥁

Genre-authentic instrumentation

Talking drum timing, shekere patterns, sebene guitar, log-drum bass, ogene bell, bougarabou percussion — each genre's sonic fingerprints are preserved, not simulated generically.

🎛️

Studio mastering included

Every output runs through a genre-tuned DSP pipeline: EQ, compression, −14 LUFS loudness normalisation, 320 kbps MP3. Upload directly to Spotify without touching a DAW.

🧬

Voice cloning (Creator+)

Apply a licensed artist voice model to AI-generated vocals using RVC v2. Artists can monetise their vocal signature. Producers can maintain a consistent artist identity across releases.

🎚️

WAV stems for remixing

Creator and Pro plans include separated stems — vocals, drums, bass, guitar — so you can remix, re-arrange, or layer in your own DAW. Full creative control.
